CHAPTER 1:Bhagavad Gita

धृतराष्ट्र उवाच |
धर्मक्षेत्रे कुरुक्षेत्रे समवेता युयुत्सवः |
मामकाः पाण्डवाश्चैव किमकुर्वत सञ्जय || १-१||

Explanation: This verse sets the scene for the entire Bhagavad Gita. It is a question posed by the blind king Dhritarashtra to his charioteer Sanjaya.

About ISKCON

ISKCON is a global spiritual organization founded in 1966 by A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada. ISKCON's mission is to spread the practice of Bhakti Yoga and bring people closer to Lord Krishna.
