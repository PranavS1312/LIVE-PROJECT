Hare Krishna!

Sri Sri Krishna Balaram Mandir

The Sri Sri Krishna Balaram Mandir, situated in the Raman Reti area of Vrindavan, holds a special significance for the devotees of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness. This grand temple represents the fulfilled dream of Srila Prabhupada, Founder Acharya of ISKCON, who wished to construct a temple of unparalleled beauty for the worship of the transcendental brothers Krishna and Balaram in the same village where Srila Prabhupada directly oversaw all aspects of its design and construction and personally invited the Lord to accept the worship of His devotees from around the world. He said, “Lord Balaram is the source of spiritual strength, and I have built this temple just to give strength to the devotees.”

About ISKCON

ISKCON is a global spiritual organization founded in 1966 by A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada. ISKCON's mission is to spread the practice of Bhakti Yoga and bring people closer to Lord Krishna.
